Capturing Golden Hour

The hour before sunset and after sunrise brings warm, soft light that adds depth and atmosphere to portraits and landscapes. Every photographer should experiment with golden hour lighting.

5 Tips for Street Photography

Look for interesting shadows, capture candid emotions, blend into the crowd, use a prime lens, and always be ready to click. Street photography is about telling raw and authentic stories.
